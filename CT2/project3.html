About This Game

Deep within the ArtCenter Making Lab, a lone Arduino board has fallen prey to a mysterious Bug invasion. As the lab's last standing system defender, you must swiftly eliminate rogue Bugs while safeguarding essential components vital to system stability.

Circuit Defender challenges your reflexes and decision-making under pressure. Strike fast to score points ‚Äî but be cautious: damaging core modules will destabilize the system and cost you precious lives.

Featuring minimalist visuals of real-world prototypes, from early cylindrical placeholders to finalized models, Circuit Defender brings a blend of physical crafting and digital gameplay to life.

Development Journey

Circuit Defender began as a simple prototype using cylindrical placeholders to represent Bugs. Through multiple iterations, including early coding failures and eventual breakthroughs, the game evolved into a fully functional reaction-based experience. Screenshots of the early prototypes, failed code versions, and successful builds capture this creative process.
